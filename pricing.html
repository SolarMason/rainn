The terms

Plain English. No fine print.

Cancellation

Free with 24+ hours' notice. Inside 24 hours, 50% of the fee applies. If we cancel inside 24 hours without an emergency, your next clean is half off.

Access

Two-hour arrival windows. If we cannot get in and no entry method was provided, the visit is billed at 50%. Keys and codes are stored securely and returned when service ends.

Weather

We reschedule for hazardous roads at no charge. Same-day Airbnb turnovers get priority.

Pets

Happy to work around them — please secure any pet aggressive toward strangers. Pet waste, litter boxes, and aquariums are add-ons.

Condition

If the property is well beyond the scope you booked, we call you before we start with a revised price. We will not silently do the extra work, or silently skip it.

Payment

Due on completion unless you are on a written account agreement. Commercial contracts: net 15.

Supplies

We bring everything. Need specific products for allergies or delicate surfaces? Leave them out and we will use yours.

Damage

If we break something you hear it from us the same day, in writing, with a photo. We carry general liability insurance. Please secure valuables before any visit.

Guarantee

Report a missed area within 24 hours and we re-clean it free. Miss twice and that portion is refunded.
